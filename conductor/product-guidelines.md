# Product Guidelines

## Content & Prose Style
- **Adaptive Tone**: The writing style is not restricted to technical documentation; it must dynamically adapt to the user's specific prompt, ranging from academic and professional to narrative and engaging.
- **Intent-Driven Structure**: The organization of content should reflect the core purpose defined in the user's initial input.

## Visual Identity
- **User-Defined Aesthetic**: The visual design and layout philosophy are driven primarily by the user's prompt, ensuring the output matches the requested "form" and context.
- **Industrial Quality**: Regardless of the specific style, the output must maintain a professional, high-quality finish with rigorous attention to typography and layout integrity.

## Interactivity & Components
- **Dynamic Interactivity**: The system utilizes "Prompt-Driven Interactivity," where the level and type of interactive elements (like galleries or step-by-step guides) are determined by the user's specific requirements.
- **Component-Level Control**: Interactivity is managed through specialized :::script controllers to ensure logic and presentation remain decoupled.
